CCF-NIFES is divided into various units aimed at growing people and moulding their destinies to become true servants of God. We'd like you to get to know our units.

Calvary Youth Singers

Calvary Youth Singers popularly known as CYS is a christian based choir that offers spirit filled rendition. Their main cause is to bring down the presence of God through worship and catalyze the atmosphere to accept the presence of God.

The Publicity Unit

The Publicity Unit is a christian based media network focused on propagating the gospel, projecting the true image of Christ, perfecting the disciples of Christ. We are the birds that sing of Christ on the tree-tops. We give updates of spiritually edifying programs to be held in CCF-NIFES and we daily feed the body of CFF-NIFES daily with edifiying contents.
